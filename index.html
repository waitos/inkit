How to Delete Your Inkit Account

At Inkit, we respect your privacy and want to give you full control over your data. Below you’ll find two ways to request deletion of your account and all associated data.

1. Direct Deletion from the App

Follow these steps within the Inkit app:

  • Go to your Profile.
  • Tap Edit Profile.
  • Tap the Delete Account button.
  • Confirm your identity (Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In).
  • Tap Confirm.

Result: Your account, uploaded tattoos, and all profile information will be automatically removed.

2. Email Deletion Request

If you prefer us to handle it manually:

  • Send an email to waitosapps@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account".
  • Include the email address associated with your Inkit account in the message body.
  • We will reply to confirm we’ve started the deletion process.

What Data Gets Deleted?

  • Uploaded tattoos.
  • Profile data: username, description, email address, and any user identifiers (OAuth IDs).
  • Metadata related to your in-app activity.

Deletion Timeline

Once you make a deletion request, we will complete it within 5 business days. You’ll receive a confirmation email when your account and data have been fully removed from our systems.

If you have any questions about this process, please contact us at waitosapps@gmail.com.
